# Contact Information

The Contact section contains all address and email information for students, as well as emergency contact details.

## Institutional Contact Information

This section displays the addresses the student has configured. Available address types include:

- **Mailing**
- **Permanent**
- **Street**
- **Billing**
- **Shipping**

<!-- ![Contact Information Page](./img/contact-info.png) -->
*Student contact information overview*

:::warning Residential Address Not Available
Residential address is currently not available in the system. Please speak to Elder Bishop or Lorran for more information on this item.
:::

## Email Addresses

You can find two types of email addresses on this page:

- **Primary Email Address** - The student's personal email
- **BYUH Email Address** - Their institutional email

## Emergency Contacts

To view emergency contacts:

1. Click the **Friends and Family** header

<!-- ![Emergency Contacts](./img/emergency-contacts.png) -->
*Emergency contacts display*

This shows all emergency contacts the student has provided, which is essential for visa documentation and emergency situations.

:::tip Using Contact Information
Always verify contact information is current when processing visa applications or sending important notifications to students.
:::
