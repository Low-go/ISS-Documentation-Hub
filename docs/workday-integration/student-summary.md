# Student Summary Page

After clicking on a student profile, the ISS office should have access to a plethora of information on the site. The initial page you land on is called **Summary**.

![Student Summary Page](images\student-summary.jpg)
*The Student Summary page overview*

## Key Information on Summary

Most relevant to our office on this initial page would be:

- **Academic Level**
- **Class Standing**
- **Primary Program of Study**

These fields provide quick insight into the student's current academic status.

## Personal Information

To access additional personal information:

1. Click **Personal** in the sidebar
2. Click **Personal Information** near the top

![Personal Information Section](images\personal-info.png)

This section displays information ISS commonly uses to cross-reference:

- **Gender**
- **Date of Birth**
- **Marital Status**

:::tip Quick Reference
Keep this information handy when verifying student records or completing visa-related documentation.
:::
