# Academic Information

The Academics section provides a comprehensive view of a student's educational objectives and current enrollment status.

## Education Overview

When you first arrive on the Academics page, you'll see a general view of the student's education objectives:

- **Program of Study**
- **Minors**
- **Completion Status** - Whether they have completed their programs or are in progress

![Academic Overview](images\academics-overview.png)
*General academic information display*

This overview gives you quick insight into what the student is studying and their progress toward degree completion.

## Current Classes

To view a student's current and upcoming course enrollment:

1. Click on **Current Classes**

![Current Classes View](images\current-classes.png)
*Course enrollment display*

This page displays:
- Classes enrolled for the **current semester**
- Classes enrolled for the **following semester**

:::tip Using Academic Information
This is particularly useful when verifying full-time enrollment status for visa purposes or when students need enrollment verification letters.
:::
